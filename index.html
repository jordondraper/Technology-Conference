Welcome to the Conference

The annual technology conference took an unexpectedly delightful turn this year as it featured none other than the beloved Muppets. Amidst the buzz of groundbreaking innovations and cutting-edge tech demos, the presence of Jim Henson's iconic characters brought an air of whimsy and joy. Attendees found themselves charmed by Kermit the Frog moderating panel discussions with his signature wit, while Miss Piggy dazzled the audience with her flair for the dramatic during keynote speeches. From Gonzo's offbeat presentations to Fozzie Bear's tech-themed comedy routines, the Muppets seamlessly integrated entertainment into the tech sphere, reminding everyone that creativity and innovation go hand in hand. This unique blend of technology and timeless characters left a lasting impression, making it a conference to remember for years to come.

Kermit the Frog

Iconic Muppet known for his gentle demeanor, leadership qualities, and signature catchphrase, "It's not easy being green."

Miss Piggy

Iconic Muppet diva, known for her glamour, confidence, and unmistakable "Moi" catchphrase, commanding attention with style and sass.
